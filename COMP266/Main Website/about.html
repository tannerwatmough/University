About Me

As mentioned in the welcome page, my name is Tanner Watmough and I am a computer science student taking the Unviersity Certificate in Computing and Information Systems at Athabasca University that I'm transferring over to a Bachelor of Computing Science at Thompson Rivers University. I previously worked as a civil servant and also as a registered nurse. I've always been interested in creating but have lacked the skills to see my creative vision fulfilled. Progrmaming gives me a way to create and build different things all through lines of code. While it might be more complex then writing stories, or drawing in some ways, it was more accessible to me as a person who grew up with technology from middle school onward.

I hope you enjoy your visit and you can learn more about what I'm currently doing and working on in my resume and projects section.
